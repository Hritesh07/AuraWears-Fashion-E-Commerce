Lookbook & campaigns

A small selection of styled looks and campaigns that show how AuraWears pieces work in real life: on streets, in classrooms, and during late evening walks.

Drop 01

Street‑ready basics

Relaxed shirts, cropped trousers, and classic sneakers shot in natural city light. The focus is on easy layering and silhouettes that move well when you are running between lectures, practice, and hangouts.

Drop 02

Summer light edits

Breezy fits using pastel tones, breathable fabrics, and simple accessories. This campaign explores outfits you can wear all day in warm weather without feeling heavy or over‑dressed.
